About

I am currently a PhD student (Oct 2022 - Present) at the Visual Information Laboratory, University of Bristol. My research is supervised by Prof. David Bull and Dr. Aaron Zhang. My research is funded by EPSRC (iCASE), BT, and the UKRI MyWorld Strength in Places Programme. My research interests include deep video compression and volumetric video compression, particularly based on neural representations.

Activities

  • Sep 2024: Our paper on "NVRC: Neural Video Representation Compression" has been accepted by NeurIPS 2024.
  • Sep 2024: Our team won first place in the End-to-End Video Compression Track (Track 2) at the 3rd Practical End-to-End Image/Video Compression Challenge in IEEE MMSP 2024.
